Services For Every Occasion

The special services we offer !!

Weddings Makeup

This classic bridal makeup style focuses on creating a timeless, elegant look. It includes a full face of makeup—foundation, contouring, highlighting, eye makeup, and lipstick—that lasts all day.

Party Makeup

Ideal for a bold and striking look, this makeup emphasizes dramatic eye makeup, bold lips, and flawless skin. Perfect for evening parties, red carpet events, or occasions where you want to make a statement.

Fashion Makeup

This type of makeup is often used in fashion magazines or photoshoots. It is highly creative and artistic, often incorporating unusual or avant-garde elements.

Photo Makeup

Studio makeup is ideal for professional photoshoots in a controlled environment with studio lighting. It focuses on creating a flawless complexion, enhanced features, and ensuring the makeup stands out under bright, harsh lights.
